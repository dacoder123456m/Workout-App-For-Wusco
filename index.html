Workout Routine

Remember to adjust the workout routine to fit your fitness level and goals, and to give your muscles time to recover between workouts. Additionally, incorporating cardio and ab exercises can help improve overall fitness and core strength.

Monday (Push)

  • Barbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for upper chest)
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for lateral delts)
  • Cable Lateral Raise: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for lateral delts)
  • Cable Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for upper chest)
  • Cable Crunches: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for abs)

Tuesday (Pull)

  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for lats)
  • Cable Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for lats)
  • Dumbbell Shrugs: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for upper traps)
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for upper traps and rear delts)
  • Plank Holds: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds (for abs)

Wednesday (Legs)

  • Barbell Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for quads and glutes)
  • Dumbbell Lunges: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for quads and glutes)
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for quads and glutes)
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for hamstrings)
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for calves)

Thursday (Push)

  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for upper chest)
  • Seated Military Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for lateral delts)
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for triceps)
  • Cable Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for upper chest)
  • Crunches: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for abs)

Friday (Pull)

  • Barbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for lats)
  • One-Arm Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for lats)
  • Barbell Shrugs: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for upper traps)
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for upper traps and rear delts)
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for abs)

Saturday (Legs)

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for glutes and hamstrings)
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (for quads and glutes)
  • Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for quads)
  • Seated Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (for calves)

Sunday (Rest)

Take a break and let your body recover.
